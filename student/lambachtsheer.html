Larissa Ambachtsheer

Lowland

Larissa Ambachtsheer is a young photographer based in The Hague, who is intrigued by the vulnerability and inabilty of people. She focuses especially on the human pain, the sadness and the desire and she aims to capture that in a intuitive way.

The digitization of communication has developed extremely in the last few years, and we now have endless possibilities. These technological advances have led to a blurred line between artificiality and nature. We have been detached from ‘real’ nature. With her project Larissa would like to invite the viewer to look differently at nature and experience it again.

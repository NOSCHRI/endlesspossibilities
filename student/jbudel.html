Jesse Budel

I belong to me
If you can’t make it, just fake it

Jesse Budel is a 21 years old photographer living in The Hague. He is inspired by theatre, the Disney world and his fascination for collecting stuff.

For his project he is realizing his biggest dream; becoming a musicalstar. Inspired by the lifes of real musicalactors, he started his own life. His whole life-faking project turned out to be a research and a reaction on the life of real musicalstars. A pleasent journey, with a criticized outcome.

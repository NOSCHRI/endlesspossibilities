Charlotte van de Zande

Memento Mori
Remember to die

Charlotte van de Zande is a 21-year-old Dutch photographer. In her work, she likes to ask questions about philosophical topics and subject, and wants people to then think about the questions that she evokes.

Charlotte’s project gives you endless possibilities in how life after death might look like. For her project Charlotte visits different people and asks them about their view on a possible afterlife. She interviews the people and most importantly; she portraits them. With the facts and stories from the people, she tries to create in image that visualises how these people think about life after death; what the afterlife might look like.

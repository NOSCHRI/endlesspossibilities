Tessa van Rijn

HUMAN MALLEABILITY
The fading line between reality and illusion.

Tessa van Rijn (1988) is a documentary photographer based in The Hague. She always starts her projects out of the inner fear and desire. A fair for alteration, but in the mean time an inner feeling of hope to discover.

With the theme ‘Endless Possibilities’ I think about the future and developments that will change the future. Human Malleability is about the fading line between reality and illusion. Nowadays scientists not only study life but also designing it. The creations they are able to make are becoming more and more realistic.

Design by Marinus Schepen, Sara Risvaag and Sepus Noordmans

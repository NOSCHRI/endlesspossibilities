Post-Smart

The upcoming generation of young children will grow up surrounded by unlimited access to and unrestrained connectivity with the rest of the world. This self-sufficient, post-internet ëGeneration Zí will be able reach beyond physical restrictions of communication, and technology will enable them to establish their own social circles, sources of entertainment and education, evolving children to be more independent of their parents at an increasingly young age.

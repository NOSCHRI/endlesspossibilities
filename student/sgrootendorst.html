Sabine Grootendorst

When disgust meets discovery
‘The concept of bugs as food’

Sabine Grootendorst is a 24 year old photographer from Haarlem, Netherlands. With relative smaller stories she try’s to tell something about big themes’s as environmental issues, next nature and the future of our planet in general.

This time she is working on a story about bugs as food. Mainly focused on the western part of the world, where eating insects and insects in general giving our the feeling of disgust. Can we change our (learned) perception about bugs? Using insects for our daily protein will have a great positive effect, for now and for the future. Everything is ready for the change, the only thing that is holding it back is our perception of bugs.

Martijn Mendel

Mommie Dearest

Martijn Mendel is a 20 year old fashion photographer interested in forms of exhibitionism and voyeurism.

His project is about the friction between showing yourself and wanting to remain your privacy. This mainly focused upon the digital world. He asks questions like: are we vulnerable or protected? is it a matter of principles, or do we really have something to hide? are companies giving us ‘just’ a great service or are they invading our privacy?

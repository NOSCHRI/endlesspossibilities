Eva Czaya

52° 5’ N, 4° 19’ O

Eva Czaya is a 24 years old photography student from Germany currently doing her exchange at the Royal Academy of Art in The Hague. Starting in a new country and city, she is inspired by its places, its people and the unknown.

In her project Eva is capturing the vague feeling of strangeness. She is interested in uncertain and odd appearing encounters which appeal to our imagination; our imagination of several, endless possible stories.

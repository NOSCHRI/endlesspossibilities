Eline Benjaminsen

Relatively

Eline Benjaminsen is a 22-year-old photographer from Oslo, Norway. Her work revolves around social and public structures and norms, where she aspires to question mainstream attitudes.

Are we chained to genetic heritage or is it our surroundings and experiences that determine our character and abilities?

Nature versus nurture is a forever-on-going sociological debate. The subject of Elines’ series is a group of thirteen half-siblings whom all share their biological father; a sperm donor.

Having grown up in different families throughout The Netherlands, they only recently got to know one another. For the project Eline is making filmed portraiture of the siblings in order to enhance mannerisms, which are character traits that are sometimes said to be genetically determined.

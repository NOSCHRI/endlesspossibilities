Wiosna van Bon

Everyware
How people protect their houses against radiation.

Wiosna van Bon is a 22-year-old photographer from The Netherlands and an explorer of the playful kind. Unusual or unique behaviour of people and groups isolated from society fascinate Wiosna and her interest in psychology and sociology are the core of her photographic research.

In her latest project about electrosensitive people, Wiosna shows us the precautions these people take to prevent physical distress and even sickness caused by electromagnetic fields, also referred to as radiation. By showing these safety measures, she reveals a problem that most people are not aware of, as we cannot see, hear, smell or touch radiation.

Mirjam Suiker

Steadfasted out

Mirjam Suiker is a photographer from Amsterdam. She is interested in shapes and colours that coincidentally form a coherent object, in physics with a narrative and in accidental interactions between people. Autumn is her favourite season, because it shows decay in the most beautiful and colourful way possible.

Does one single truth exist or might multiple explanations be possible, either visible or invisible? Thinking you know for sore what the facts are, being sceptical and then suddenly experiencing something which turns all your believes upside down. This is something that can happen to anybody.

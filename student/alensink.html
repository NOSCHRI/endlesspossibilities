Anita Lensink

Wandering

Anita Lensink is a photographic poet, who works with themes like tranquility and vulnerability. She often finds her subjects in the relationship between human, animal and nature.

Trying to escape the hectic world of today in which everything is possible she goes outside. In “Wandering” we follow her to find rest and stillness in nature.

Nita Vera

Between the Acts

Nita Vera is a 28-year-old Finnish photographer. She uses staged photography exploring femininity in our society and relationships between people.

Her project consists of small scenes. Staging daily life moments in domestic environments, Nita focuses on invisible aspects with a psychological atmosphere and in this she investigate how women are being perceived.

She approaches this topic by showing women in ordinary situations that evoke an intense moment.

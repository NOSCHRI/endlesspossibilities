Annemiek Schout

WANNABE -15, 14, 13...-

Annemiek Schout is a 24-year-old photographer who is inspired by people, the way they present themselves and play with identity.

Platforms like Youtube, Facebook and an overload of talent shows made our desire for visibility bigger than ever. More and more people want to be acknowledged, appreciated and admired by the masses. Fame is not only the result of talent or achievements; for a lot of people it is a goal itself. A 'Wannabe' is a person who wishes to be or do something, but lacks the talent or qualifications. A person who copies a look, speech and mannerism to impress the audience, if only for 15 minutes. I poke fun at these wannabe stars in a series of over the top glossy studio photos, filled with inauthenticity and amateurism. Hurry up! Time is ticking and fame is fleeting...

Lisa van Casand

Fluorescent signals

Lisa van Casand is a 24 years old Dutch photographer who is inspired by the fine line between science and the imaginary. Scientific discoveries have always nourished her thought and have served as an incentive to start fantasizing about what will be next.

For this project she researched the recent developments in plant science that show that plants are not static, decorative objects, but complex organisms. When in stress, plants emit a light which is invisible to our eyes. By measuring this fluorescent emission she hopes to shed a new light on the world of plants.

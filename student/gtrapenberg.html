Gilleam Trapenberg

Mafia Music

Gilleam Trapenberg is a 22 year old photographer from Curacao. In his work he is often inspired by the influence of the American culture, identity and status.

In his most recent project he focusses on the Hip-Hop culture and specifically focusses on Dutch Caribbean men currently living in the Netherlands. Status is a significant factor in this culture, he is interested in these stereotypes that surround gangster-rap music and how these men portray themselves.

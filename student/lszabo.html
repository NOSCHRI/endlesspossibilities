Liza Szabó

Would you walk with me

Liza Szabó is a 22-year-old Hungarian photographer who is inspired by people, their personalities and the connection towards reality.

She is interested in relationships, and according to her there are endless possibilities to connect and communicate with someone. In her project she searches for the diversity of connection between couples that live together. She investigates how different couples get along with each other and in what state their relationship is.

Liza Szabó

Would you walk with me

Liza Szabó is a 22 years old Hungarian photographer who is inspired by people, their personality and connection towards reality.

She is interested in relationships and in her point of view there are endless possibilities to connect and communicate with someone. In her project she searches for the diversity of connection between couples that live together. She investigates how different couples come along and in which state their relationship are.

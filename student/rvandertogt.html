Renate van der Togt

Let’s Hang

Renate Ariadne van der Togt is a 22 year old photographer interested in the photographical fine line between fiction and reality. Abstaining from the studio she combines people and objects with places and surroundings that don’t seem to belong together or match too well.

The past year the news continuously updated us on issues regarding conflicts between cultural groups within the Netherlands. The black Piet discussion and Wilders’ statements about Moroccans are two examples of this. In this project Renate tries to shine light upon this development by showing us a fictional group of friends of different cultural backgrounds.

Different settings with combinations of people dressed in the fashion of young designers show the strangeness and awkwardness of their combinations but it also shows beauty as well as a promise.

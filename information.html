ENDLESS
POSSIBILITIES

For four months 39 photography students of the Royal Academy of Art in The Hague will explore the scope and elasticity of the theme Endless Possibilities. From 16 until the 25th of January they will present their results in an exhibition they themselves will organise and build from scratch.

Through exchange of process and ideas the students will collaborate with ECAL/Ecole Cantonale d’Art de Lausanne, Switzerland. Furthermore a team of specialists including Hans van der Meer, Krista van der Niet, Arie Versluijs, Raimond Wouda, Judith van IJken, Pawel Pokutycki en Jan Frederik Groot will guide them through this intensive trajectory. Besides photography film and interactive media will play an important part of their final presentations.

The young professionals will dive into a variety of subject from both a documentary and a fictional perspective, exploring and analysing various unknown domains. Their projects vary from personal stories from a Mars-mission candidate to research and studies of human connection and interaction in a time where it’s all about the individual. By asking critical questions they’ll expose both the endless and limited possibilities on a social, political and technical level, in which their own position as an individual, group and world citizen is key.
